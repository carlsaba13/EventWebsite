General Information

Venue: Banc of California Stadium
Capacity: 22,000
Address: 3939 S Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90037

Elevators

Banc of California Stadium is equipped with a total of 5 public elevators at the following 3 locations:

  • VIP Lobby (3 total)
  • Behind Section 101
  • Behind Section 116

Entrances

There are a total of seven (7) entrances to Banc of California Stadium.

  • Grand Lobby Entrance in the Pepsi Plaza on the Northwest side of the Stadium
  • Grand Lobby Entrance on the Southwest side of the Stadium
  • Toyota Gate located on the Northwest side of the Stadium
  • North Gates
  • Northeast Gates
  • Figueroa (East) Gates
  • Southeast Gates

Family Restrooms

Family restrooms are designed for the use of fans with small children and fans with disabilities. These restrooms are located in the following locations:

  • Section 102
  • Section 106
  • Section 110
  • Section 116
  • Section 121
  • Section 123
  • Section 129
  • Section 131
  • Section 202
  • Sunset Club Level South Suite Hallway

Suites

There are 35 suites at Banc of California Stadium in the following locations:

  • 10 Field Level Suites
  • 12 Founders Level Suites
  • 2 Party Suites on the Founders Level
  • 10 Sunset Deck Suites
  • 1 Party Suite on the Sunset Deck
